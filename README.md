Building a small game for the world

WorldSnap 🌍 
— A daily photo guessing game where you and your friends around the world get the same mystery location photo every day. 
You have 3 attempts to guess the country, with hints revealed after each wrong answer. Compare results, build your streak, and share your score. 
A new challenge every day — no accounts, no downloads, just open and play.
